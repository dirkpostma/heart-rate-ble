PROTOTYPE — the three faces

Three variants of the Dynamic Island (minimal / compact / expanded), Lock Screen Live Activity, and small home-screen widget. Flip with the bar below or ← / → keys. The Live / Stale toggle shows the agreed stale treatment: dimmed BPM, relative age, pulse stopped. Data shown is exactly what the app has: current BPM, device name, connection state, reading age (variant B additionally assumes the app keeps the last ~40 readings in memory for a sparkline).

A — Digits

Typographic. The number is the whole interface; the heart is a small live indicator. Fastest possible read at a glance, most honest about how little data there is.

# Privacy Policy — Heart Rate BLE

_Last updated: July 14, 2026_

Heart Rate BLE ("the app") is developed by Dirk Postma. This policy describes how the app handles your information.

## The short version

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your heart rate never leaves your device.

## What the app does

Heart Rate BLE connects to a Bluetooth Low Energy heart-rate sensor that you choose (such as a chest strap or a sports watch in broadcast mode) and displays the live heart-rate readings on your device, including in a Live Activity on the Lock Screen and in a home-screen widget.

## Data collection

- **No data is collected.** Heart-rate readings are displayed in real time and are not saved, logged, or transmitted anywhere.
- **No accounts.** The app has no sign-up, login, or user profiles.
- **No analytics or tracking.** The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or trackers.
- **No third-party sharing.** Since nothing is collected, nothing is shared.

## Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth solely to scan for and connect to nearby heart-rate sensors implementing the standard Bluetooth Heart Rate Service. Bluetooth access is used only for this purpose.

## App updates

The app may download over-the-air updates of its own code and assets (via Expo Updates, operated by Expo/650 Industries) to keep the app current. These requests do not include personal information beyond what is technically necessary to serve the update (such as the app's version and platform).
